About COCO

Your Trusted Partner in Bali Real Estate

COCO Development Group is a premier real estate investment company specialising in luxury villas and premium properties across Bali. Founded by Rasmus Holst, we have built an unrivalled reputation for connecting international investors with exceptional opportunities in one of the world's most desirable destinations.

Our team of over 50 professionals — combining deep local insight with international financial expertise — provides end-to-end guidance: from your first enquiry through to achieving outstanding rental yields and long-term capital growth.
